## ğŸ® How to Play

### Your Turn Actions

1. **See Cards** (Optional)
   - Click to reveal your hand
   - Costs chips but helps make better decisions
   - Bots can see you've seen your cards

2. **Fold**
   - Give up your hand
   - Lose your current bet
   - Safe option with weak hands

3. **Call**
   - Match the current bet
   - Stay in the round
   - Good for decent hands

4. **Raise**
   - Increase the bet
   - Forces others to match or fold
   - Use with strong hands or to bluff

### Winning

- **Last Player Standing**: If all others fold, you win!
- **Showdown**: Best hand wins at the end
- **Pot**: Winner takes all chips in the pot

## ğŸ¯ Game Flow

1. **Deal**: Each player gets 3 cards
2. **Blind**: Dealer pays blind (ante)
3. **Betting Round**: Players fold, call, or raise
4. **Showdown**: Remaining players reveal cards
5. **Winner**: Best hand takes the pot
6. **Next Round**: Dealer rotates, repeat
